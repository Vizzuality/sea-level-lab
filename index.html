Sea rise
level experiment

This data experiment explores the potential impacts of different sea level rise scenarios for 5 coastal cities: Barcelona, San Francisco, Boston, San Sebastian and Aarhus. Using 1m resolution DEMs, these maps show how much land, and which areas, may become flooded for a given sea level rise, assuming no change in elevation due to accretion or subsidence and no new sea level defences. For more information on how we did it, visit blog.vizzuality.com.
